About the Tafsir App

Welcome to our Tafsir App! This app is designed to help you better understand the Quran. Our key features include:

  • Mufassir Biographies: Information about renowned tafsir scholars.
  • Tafsir Sources: Various tafsir sources from classical to contemporary.
  • Quran Mushaf: Complete Quran text with diacritics and tajweed.
  • Tafsir Methods: Explanations of the tafsir methods used by scholars.

Thank you for using our app.
